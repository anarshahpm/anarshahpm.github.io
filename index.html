About

"The best products are built by leaders who stay close to the customer, move fast with data, and never lose sight of the business outcome."

I have shipped products used by hundreds of millions of people at Amazon, Yahoo!, eBay, and MakerPlace by Michaels Stores — and I am currently building Sycamore, a stealth AI venture, from zero.
